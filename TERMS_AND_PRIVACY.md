## 3. PRIVACY POLICY

### 3.1 Information We Collect

**Personal Information:**
- Email address (@ue.edu.ph)
- Nickname/Display name
- College and year level
- Gender and preferences
- Profile photos (2 maximum)
- Hobbies and bio description
- Swipe history and match data
- Chat messages and task completions

**Automatically Collected:**
- Device information and browser type
- IP address and location data
- Usage statistics and interaction patterns
- Login timestamps and activity logs

### 3.2 How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to:
- Create and manage your profile
- Match you with compatible users
- Facilitate communication between matches
- Improve app functionality and user experience
- Ensure safety and enforce community guidelines
- Prevent fraud and abuse
- Comply with legal obligations

**We will NEVER:**
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use your photos for marketing without consent
- Disclose your information except as required by law

### 3.3 Data Sharing and Disclosure

**With Other Users:**
- Your profile information is visible to approved users
- Your photos, bio, and preferences are shown in the matching system
- Chat messages are private between matched users only

**With Third Parties:**
- Supabase (our infrastructure provider) for data storage
- Only when required by law or to protect safety

**We Do NOT Share:**
- Your email address with other users
- Your swipe history or rejected matches
- Private messages with anyone except the recipient
- Your data with marketing companies

### 3.4 Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
- Secure authentication systems
- Regular security audits and updates
- Access controls and monitoring
- Automatic logout after inactivity

**However, no system is 100% secure.** You acknowledge that:
- Internet transmission has inherent risks
- You use the App at your own risk
- You should never share sensitive information in chats

### 3.5 Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your data
- **Correction**: Update incorrect information
- **Deletion**: Request account and data removal
- **Portability**: Export your data
- **Objection**: Opt-out of certain data processing
- **Restriction**: Limit how we use your data

**To exercise these rights, contact**: admin@ueheart.app (placeholder)

### 3.6 Data Retention

- **Active accounts**: Data retained while account is active
- **Deleted accounts**: Data removed within 30 days
- **Legal requirements**: Some data may be retained for legal compliance
- **Chat history**: Deleted permanently when match is unmatched
- **Reported content**: Retained for investigation purposes (90 days)

### 3.7 Cookies and Tracking

We use:
- **Essential cookies**: Required for authentication and functionality
- **Analytics**: To understand usage patterns (anonymized)
- **No advertising cookies**: We don't track you for ads

You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

### 4.1 Profile Accuracy
- Keep your profile information current and truthful
- Use recent photos that accurately represent you
- Do not misrepresent your identity, college, or status

### 4.2 Safety and Security
- Never share your password with anyone
- Be cautious when meeting matches in person
- Meet in public places for first dates
- Tell a friend where you're going

- Do not share financial information

### 4.3 Photo Guidelines

## 5. MATCHING AND SWIPES

### 5.1 How Matching Works
- You see profiles of users who match your gender preferences
- Users who match your gender preferences can see you
- A "match" occurs when both users swipe right
- Matches enable chat and couple missions

### 5.2 Swipe Etiquette
- Swipe decisions are final and cannot be undone
- Do not create fake accounts to re-match
- Respect the matching algorithm
- No harassment if someone doesn't match with you

## 6. CHAT AND MESSAGING

### 6.1 Message Guidelines
- Keep conversations respectful and appropriate
- No harassment, threats, or offensive language
- No spam or promotional content
- No sharing of explicit images
- Respect if someone doesn't want to chat

### 6.2 Couple Missions (Tasks)
- Complete missions together for fun bonding activities
- Missions are optional - no obligation
- Report inappropriate mission suggestions
- Do not use missions to pressure or manipulate

### 6.3 Message Privacy
- Messages are private between matched users
- Admins may review flagged conversations
- Do not screenshot or share private conversations without consent
- Legal authorities may access messages if required by law
