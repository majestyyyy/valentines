## 5. PLATFORM FEATURES & FUNCTIONALITY

### 5.1 Profile Creation & Setup

**Required Information:**
- Verified @ue.edu.ph email
- Nickname (3-20 characters)
- Gender and gender preference
- College and year level
- At least 1 profile photo
- Looking for category

**Optional Information:**
- Bio (up to 500 characters)
- Hobbies (up to 3)
- Additional profile photo

**Profile Approval:**
- All new profiles reviewed by admin
- Approval typically within 24-48 hours
- May request additional verification
- Profile visible only after approval

### 5.2 Matching System

**How Matching Works:**
1. System shows users who match your gender preference
2. You appear to users whose preference includes your gender
3. Swipe right to like, left to pass
4. Match created when both users swipe right
5. Chat unlocked for matched pairs

**Matching Criteria:**
- Gender preferences (primary filter)
- Active account status
- Approved profile only
- Not previously blocked/reported by you
- Within your college/campus (optional filter)

**Match Limitations:**
- No limit on total matches
- Can unmatch anytime
- Cannot re-match after unmatch (without appeal)
- Blocked users never appear again

### 5.3 Chat & Messaging

**Features:**
- Real-time messaging between matches
- Send text messages (max 1000 characters)
- Emoji support
- Read receipts
- Typing indicators
- Message history

**Limitations:**
- Chat only available with active matches
- No photo/video/file sharing (safety measure)
- No voice or video calls
- No external link sharing (to prevent scams)

**Best Practices:**
- Keep conversation appropriate
- Don't share personal contact info immediately
- Get to know match before meeting
- Report inappropriate messages

### 5.4 Secret Admirer Feature

**How It Works:**
- Someone who liked you appears with blurred photo
- Shows their college (e.g., "Someone likes you from CCSS")
- Keep swiping to potentially discover them
- No pressure to accept or connect

**Privacy:**
- Identity hidden until mutual match
- No action buttons to avoid pressure
- Encourages organic discovery
- Can still swipe left if you see them later

## 8. REPORTING, MODERATION & ENFORCEMENT

### 8.1 Reporting System

**What to Report:**
- Harassment, bullying, or threats
- Inappropriate photos or content
- Fake profiles or catfishing
- Spam or scam attempts
- Underage users
- Terms of service violations
- Safety concerns
- Suspicious behavior

**How to Report:**

**From Profile:**
1. Click three dots on user's profile
2. Select "Report User"
3. Choose violation category
4. Provide details (optional but helpful)
5. Submit report

**From Chat:**
1. Click flag icon in chat
2. Select reason
3. Add context
4. Submit

**Anonymity:**
- Reports are completely anonymous
- Reported user does NOT know who reported them
- Multiple reports trigger automatic review

### 8.2 Admin Review Process

**Timeline:**
- Initial review: Within 24 hours
- Investigation: 1-3 business days
- Decision notification: Via email

**Investigation Includes:**
- Reviewing reported content
- Checking user history
- Examining chat logs (if relevant)
- Considering context and pattern
- Evaluating severity

**Possible Outcomes:**
- No violation found (no action)
- Warning issued
- Content removed
- Temporary suspension (7 days)
- Permanent ban
- Report to university authorities (severe cases)

### 8.3 Enforcement Levels

**Level 1: Warning**
- First minor offense
- User notified via email
- Content removed
- Required to acknowledge guidelines
- No access restriction

**Examples:**
- Inappropriate but not explicit photo
- Minor guideline misunderstanding
- Unintentional spam behavior

**Level 2: Temporary Suspension**
- Second offense or moderate violation
- 7-day account suspension
- All matches paused
- Cannot access platform during suspension
- Required to read guidelines before reactivation

**Examples:**
- Repeated inappropriate messages after warning
- Multiple user reports
- Borderline harassment
- Spam after warning

**Level 3: Permanent Ban**
- Third offense or severe violation
- Account permanently deleted
- All data removed
- Email blocked from re-registration
- Cannot appeal (severe cases)

**Examples:**
- Explicit sexual content
- Threats of violence
- Doxxing or sharing private info
- Impersonation or fraud
- Repeated violations after suspension

**Immediate Ban (No Warning):**
- Illegal activity
- Child endangerment
- Revenge porn or non-consensual images
- Death threats or violence
- Harassment campaign
- Commercial prostitution

### 8.4 Appeals Process

**If You Believe Ban Was Unjust:**
1. Email noreply.uewelfare.official@gmail.com within 14 days
2. Subject: "Ban Appeal - [Your Email]"
3. Provide:
   - Your account email
   - Date of ban
   - Reason given (if known)
   - Your explanation
   - Any supporting evidence

**Review:**
- Independent admin reviews appeal
- Decision within 7 business days
- Decision is final
- If successful, account reinstated with warning

**No Appeals For:**
- Illegal activity
